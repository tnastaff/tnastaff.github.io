Oddworld: Story Stones IV - GLOKTIGI

While the average inhabitant might believe that Industrialists have denounced
their spirituality and dedicated the entirety of their existence to science and
technology, this is actually far from the truth. Behind closed doors, the
wealthiest and most powerful members of the Industrialist clans dabble in
alchemy and the dark art of the occult. These lend them supernatural powers and
even mastery over life as evidenced by Gloktigi, bizarre hybrids birthed by
Glukkon queens and fathered by high ranking Oktigi, held together by alchemy and
genetics.[1]

The Gloktigi embody Oddworld's version of Djinns, evil spirits bound by
contracts to do their master's bidding. Standing over fifteen feet tall, they
reach the height of the fearsome Vykker security robots, the Snoozers.[2]

By tapping into their occult powers[3], they have the ability to spontaneously
become translucent and non-corporeal, which lets them ignore gravity and freely
pass through solid objects. Also they are able to eject a green, sticky
substance, that wraps around their victim, incapatitaing them. Anyone
unfortunate enough to be caught in it is then promptly dispatched either by the
creature's shots or it's razor sharp talons.

These particular skills coupled with their extreme durability make them
excellent bodyguards for Industrialist VIPs, who are frequently seen with one
or two of Gloktigi at their side.

Stranger meets four of these beings who have been cybernetically enchanced,
while invading Sekto Springs. [4] The creatures, whom Sekto refers to as his
pets never seem to utter a word, their vocalization consisting only of pig-like
squeals. One might believe that they are animals guided solely by their
instincts, however, in reality Gloktigi are intelligent and cunning, but also
terribly egoistical. For them any other creature is so inferior, that they
won't even tire themselves with trying to speak to them. They also tend to
undergo fits of violence, where they can't contain their demonic frenzy.
Despite this, they are still highly-trained and loyal to their masters. [5]

However, a beast of such immense power faces a relatively short life. Their
average lifespan of 40 years [6] means they live about as long as a Mudokon
drone does [7].
