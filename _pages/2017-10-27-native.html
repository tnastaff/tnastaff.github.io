The Native species of Oddworld live in respectful balance with the land. Their lives are based around Spooce, a sort of spiritual currency earned by channeling energies from living creatures. They are intuitive and practical, and believe that what goes around, comes around. Their simple needs and honest approach to life makes them prey to rapacious Industrial races.
