Date: September 2004

Source: GMR, Issue 20, p. 26

Developer Oddworld Inhabitants, best known for Munch's Oddysee at the Xbox launch, has announced a new publisher for its latest game. Electronic Arts will publish Stranger, assumed to be a casualty of the game bloodbath that followed Ed Fries' departure from MS early this year. It will appear on Xbox and PS2 in 2005.
