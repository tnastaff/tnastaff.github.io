Oddworld: The Lost Archives is an ongoing release schedule of archived Oddworld concept art from veterans Farzad Varahramyan, Steven Olds, Gautam Babbar, Silvio Aebischer and Raymond Swanland. The artworks, complete with commentary from Oddworld Inhabitants, are currently being released every weekday on the Oddworld Inhabitants Instagram account. The current plan is to release a new image every weekday until the release of Oddworld: Soulstorm.
