The Daily Deception is a propaganda newspaper featured heavily in Munch's Oddysee, detailing the disastrous and often humorous escapades of Abe and Munch's adventures and their effects upon the Magog Cartel.

The newspaper was resurrected by Oddworld Inhabitants for several short issues to keep fans invested in the franchise between the prolonged wait between Munch's Oddysee and Stranger's Wrath. Each issue is brimming with wry wit, taking fans on a short sojourn back to Oddworld.
