Name: Exclusive Amazon Orange Abe Skin [version 1.0.3]
Date: 2 November, 2017

Source: https://www.amazon.com/dp/B017YH91Z6/ref=s9_acss_bw_cg_rzex_1b1_w?pf_rd_m=ATVPDKIKX0DER&pf_rd_s=merchandised-search-1&pf_rd_r=J546WVYTFJWDMV2JKEYZ&pf_rd_t=101&pf_rd_p=4e8a730d-f5ce-462c-a8fd-f6fdf6607a65&pf_rd_i=17247045011

This exclusive skin was a limited feature for the Amazon version of Munch's Oddysee as part of large, curated sale. The orange skin tone was added to the game to reflect Amazon's video game marketing department, which predominately uses orange in their products. This feature was implemented by Square One—the team responsible for finally gaining access to the source code for Abe's Oddysee—and was endorsed by Oddworld Inhabitants (according to Alex Carroll).
