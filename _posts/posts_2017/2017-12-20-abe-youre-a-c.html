Oddmeme History

The "Abe, You're a C***" meme was created in 2016 by the Oddworld Forums user Nepsotic in response to a proposed role-play thread from AbeMudokonPop8 where forums members would ask Abe a question. The rather absurd rules dictated by AbeMudokonPop8, however, and the general lack of imagination or ingenuity quickly resulted in forum members turning the thread into a joke, spinning ridiculous questions and throwing gratuitous insults at the character of Abe.

The joke quickly exploded, fuelled by Oddworld Forums Discord users Paul and Phoetux, who began generating and spamming popular memes adjusted to include variations of "Abe, You're a Cunt".
