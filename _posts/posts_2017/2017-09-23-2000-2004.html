Dear Alf was the main means by which Oddworld Inhabitants communicated with their fans during the first half of the 2000s. Fans would submit questions and Alf—one of Abe's fellow mudokon survivors from RuptureFarms, and the proprietor of Alf's Rehab & Tea from the end of Abe's Exoddus—would provide answers that were often sardonic and open to interpretation.

With 39 original issues of Dear Alf, ranging from October, 2000 – December, 2004, a wealth of Oddworld's greater lore has been derived from these prodigiously cheesy and oh-so-rememberable letters.
