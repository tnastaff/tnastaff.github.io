Typical of the Oddworld's Native species are the Mudokon. Chumps, slaves, monks, and food stock all in one, the Mudokons were the dominant species of Oddworld. . . until the Magog Cartel enslaved them to work in their Franchises. Far away in the wilderness, where Sligs rarely tread, free Mudokons preserve the old ways, speaking in their melodious tongue, and moving mountains with psychic chants.
