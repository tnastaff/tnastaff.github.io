Deceased denizens of the sacred city of Necrum, the Mudokon Spirit Guides were roused from their comfortable slumber by greedy Glukkon bone ranchers who have been plundering Mudokon graves for the main addictive ingredient for SoulStorm™ Brew. After granting Abe mystical powers and helping him to destroy SoulStorm Brewery, the Mudokon Spirit Guides returned to their crypt where they are still kvetching about Abe’s performance.
