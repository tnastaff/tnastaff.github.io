With the partnership between Oddworld Inhabitants and JAW, our favourite sarcastic mudokon was reappointed as Editor-in-Chief of his eponymous letter/reply service. This resurrected version of Dear Alf followed the same procedural beats as the original, offering a sprinkle of lore hidden within tongue-in-cheek retorts.

After the release of New 'n' Tasty and the amicable split between Oddworld Inhabitants and JAW, Dear Alf was once again retired, much to the displeasure of the wider fandom.
