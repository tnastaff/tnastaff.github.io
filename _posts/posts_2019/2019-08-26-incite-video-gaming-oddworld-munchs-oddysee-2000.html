Date: January 2000

Source: Incite Video Gaming, Issue 2, p. 24

INCOMING Checking the latest games out in the coming months, one hit at a time.

ODDWORLD: MUNCH'S ODDYSEE

GT Interactive (PS2)

Release TBA

STARRING

Good old Abe, and Munch who, like Abe, is an unlikely hero also "hanging at the bottom of the food chain and slipping fast."

MISSION

You play Munch and Abe, who have different abilities, so you'll need to figure out which can achieve immediate goals.

THOUGHTS

LORNE LANNING

"The staggering degree of life which populate Munch's world will ultimately shatter the perception of what constitutes a gaming experience."
