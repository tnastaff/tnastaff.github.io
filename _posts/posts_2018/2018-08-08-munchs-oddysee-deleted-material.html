The Harvester

Date: 06/05/2016

Official Description: The Harvester - concept art for an industrial tree-eater that didn't make it into Oddworld: Munch's Oddysee.

Source: https://twitter.com/oddworldinc/status/728510223850287104

Date: 20/04/2017

Official Description: The Oddworld: Soulstorm team chat is a fountain of amazing information. #FunFact #ThingsYourBossSays

Transcript: Munch was originally a experimental lab tape worm. Abe swallowed him to help him escape. Abe would then puke him up whenever he needed a swimmer. For some reason... we decided it may not be charming enough.

Source: https://twitter.com/OddworldInc/status/855144067906375681

Munch's Alter-Ego

Date: 06/08/2018

Official Description: Munch was originally going to have an 'alter ego' - this lovely looking fella - but sadly it didn't make the final release; he was supposed to be more of a Jekyll and Hyde character. Abe could slap him and get him mad, which provoked him and he'd turn into this maniacal killer!

Source: https://twitter.com/OddworldInc/status/1026508620203212801

Notes

  • OWI's response to question asking if this ability was specific to Munch, or something all Gabbits possess: "Specific to Munch, it was due to all the experimentation the Vykkers did on him ..."
