The Oddworld game manuals were not only helpful and informative booklets, which acclimatised players to the wonderful world of Odd, they also teased a depth of untold history and tantalising lore. A mandatory addition to the Oddworld experience, it was through these exciting manuals that we first learned about the Quintology, the hapless Munch and even the elusive Squeek.
